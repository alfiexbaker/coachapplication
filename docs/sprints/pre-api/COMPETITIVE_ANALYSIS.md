# Competitive Analysis: How We Beat Spond

## The Market Today

The market is split into two disconnected worlds:

1. **Team management** (Spond, Heja, Teamer) — free, communication + logistics, treats coaching as a volunteer hobby
2. **Booking/business platforms** (ClassForKids, OpenPlay) — paid, revenue + discovery, treats coaching as a business

**No single app bridges both.** A coach currently needs Spond for team comms AND ClassForKids for bookings AND CoachNow for development tools AND TeamSnap+ for session content.

## Our Position

> "Spond helps you manage your team. We help you grow your coaching business."

We are not Spond. We don't compete on free team RSVP. We compete on:
- **Parents find YOU** (discovery marketplace)
- **Parents book YOU** (booking engine with cash/online payment)
- **You track player development** (notes, badges, progress, video)
- **You grow your business** (branded profile, promo codes, analytics, earnings)
- **You run your club/academy professionally** (branding, calendar, events, squad management)

## Spond's Biggest Weaknesses (Our Opportunities)

From real user reviews (Trustpilot, G2, Capterra, App Store):

1. **Notification chaos** — multiple kids/teams flood with unlabelled notifications
2. **No search** — can't search messages, events, posts
3. **No per-player stats** — coaches want private notes alongside lineups
4. **No coaching tools** — no session plans, no drills, no tactical content
5. **No marketplace** — invisible to new parents
6. **Generic** — not football-specific, nothing feels tailored
7. **Payment holdbacks** — 10+ day payout delays
8. **No customer support** — chatbots only

## The 4 Things That Make Coaches Switch

### 1. BUSINESS GROWTH (they can't get new clients on Spond)
- Public coach profile that parents can Google
- Shareable booking link ("Send this to parents")
- Trial/taster sessions to convert new parents
- Promo codes for first session discounts
- Referral rewards

### 2. PROFESSIONAL TOOLS (Spond treats them as volunteers)
- Session plan templates (football-specific)
- Drill library with video demos
- Post-session notes that parents actually see
- Badge/achievement system for players
- Revenue tracking and earnings analytics

### 3. PLAYER DEVELOPMENT (Spond has zero)
- Visual skill progression (radar charts, trend lines)
- Goal setting with milestones
- Video feedback with annotations
- Homework/drill assignments
- Progress reports parents can share

### 4. CLUB IDENTITY (Spond clubs are boring)
- Branded club pages with logo, colours, cover photo
- Auto-generated match result cards in feed
- Club calendar aggregating all squads
- Achievement celebrations in the feed
- Photo gallery from sessions and matches
