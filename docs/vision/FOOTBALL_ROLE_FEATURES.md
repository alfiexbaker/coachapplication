# Football-First Feature Matrix

## Player / Athlete Experience
- **Discovery tuned to football**: onboarding only asks about positions, preferred foot, and key focuses (Dribbling, Passing, Defending, Goalkeeping, Conditioning).
- **Objectives-first booking**: players can set or adjust objectives at booking time and pin them atop every conversation and booking detail card.
- **Session history timeline**: swipeable cards for each completed session show drill focus, clips placeholder, and achievement badges that gamify returning to the platform.
- **Always-on messaging**: chat entry point is available from the player home header and each session card; quick templates let players confirm kit, field, or questions.
- **Progress analytics preview**: sparkline showing confidence trend per skill area encourages continued training and provides intrinsic motivation.

## Parent Experience
- **Multi-child switcher**: persistent pill control in the app bar toggles between kids; each context remembers filters, coaches, and objectives.
- **Unified actions hub**: dashboard cards call out items like "Confirm Saturday attendance" or "Update dribbling goal" so parents never miss a task.
- **Coach relationship center**: parents can favorite coaches, schools, or clinics and receive alerts when new availability or camps are posted.
- **Messaging concierge**: parents can message any booked coach (or the school admin) with contextual templates ("Running late", "Need equipment list").
- **Payments placeholder hooks**: while Stripe is deferred, parents still see transparent pricing, credit tracker, and upcoming bundle reminders to build trust.

## Coach Experience
- **School identity**: coaches can create a branded school profile to showcase facilities, assistant coaches, and packaged programs.
- **Service + availability wizard**: UI emphasizes football contexts (pitch location, surface type, weather contingencies) and supports per-service objectives recommendations.
- **Player insight feed**: prior to each session, coaches review the player's objective history and send prep notes via messaging.
- **Messaging with guardrails**: read receipts and quick reactions keep threads lightweight while preserving auditability for safeguarding.
- **Analytics snapshot**: coaches see bookings segmented by skill focus (dribbling, passing, defending) to understand demand and tailor offerings.

## Shared UI Patterns
- **Football-only taxonomy**: all chips, filters, and icons refer exclusively to football concepts until multi-sport support returns.
- **Pinned objectives**: appear across booking detail, messaging threads, and session history to keep everyone aligned on goals.
- **Role-based navigation**: player, parent, and coach tabs surface the actions most relevant to that persona without overwhelming others.
- **Cross-role messaging**: a floating "Message" button appears whenever a relationship exists, ensuring high responsiveness.
