# Team Hub Plan

## Badge and sharing rules
- Coaches own badge issuance and can attach awards to specific sessions; athletes and parents see the context but cannot edit.
- Parents/supporters have read-only access to badges in progress views; the only action available is a controlled "Share to profile" chip from notifications.
- Sharing a badge marks the notification handled, updates the award as shared for supporter timelines, and does not publish publicly outside the family/supporter hub.
